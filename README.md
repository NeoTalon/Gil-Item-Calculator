# Gil-Item-Calculator by NeoTalon.
Calculates the amount of Gil you have based on your Allag Pieces (from Tin to Platinum), Clear Demimateria (1 to 3) and even Moonstones.

This is for the MMORPG Final Fantasy XIV. 

In the game there are Items called Allagan Pieces which only have the purpose of being sold at NPCs for that game's ingame currency (Gil), these are mostly obtained via quests, Desynthesis on Fishes, Drops from lootboxes obtained via certain content, they range from Tin (sells for 25) to Platinum (sells for 10.000).

There is also Clear Demimateria which is obtained via Desynthesis on Furniture or Equipment, and also from Lootboxes via certain content as well, they range from Demimateria I (Sells for 200) to Demimateria III (Sells for 5000)

And there are also Moonstones, which actually do have some purpose for certain Vendors to exchange items with, and for Lucis Tools & a step for the Anima Relics.
They can be obtained via daily Ixal hand-in quests (they give 1 Moonstone), Drops from Treasure Maps, and can be bought from Grand Company shops for 4.000 Grand Company Seals. These can be sold for 1.200 Gil.

For the most part these items main use is just being sold (with the exception of Moonstones) for Gil, however on Free Trial they can be useful as a method of saving Gil once you finally subscribe to the game, due to the Free Trial's 300.000 Gil Cap.

This C# calculator will help with checking how much Gil you've saved up using these items.
